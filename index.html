The Cougar Confidence Index

Our proprietary destination scoring system — built for grown women, by grown women.

Every destination earns a Cougar Confidence Index score from 1–100, based on nine factors that matter most to women who travel with self-knowledge and standards. This isn't TripAdvisor. This isn't a "best beaches" listicle. This is a systematic evaluation of how a destination treats women like you — with the respect, safety, and warmth you deserve.

  • Respect for Women How women are treated in public spaces, restaurants, hotels, and streets. Eye contact. Tone. The absence of harassment.
  • Safety Reality Street safety, harassment frequency, scam risk, neighborhood variation. What it actually feels like to walk alone.
  • Social Ease How easy it is to meet people naturally — at cafes, in classes, at markets. Whether solo dining feels comfortable or conspicuous.
  • Cultural Depth Museums, neighborhoods, history, street art, local traditions. The kind of richness that rewards repeat visits and slow exploration.
  • Wellness Access Spas, thermal baths, yoga, walkability, farmers' markets, and the kind of slow-life infrastructure that takes care of your body.
  • Solo-Friendliness Bar seating for one. Walking tours with no couples energy. Hotel staff who don't pity you. A city that makes alone feel like freedom.
  • Age Inclusivity How 40/50/60+ women are perceived and welcomed. Whether maturity earns respect or invisibility. Cultural attitudes toward aging.
  • Healthcare Access Hospital quality, pharmacy accessibility, English-speaking doctors, travel insurance compatibility. The practical stuff that matters.
  • Ease Score Walkability, transit confidence, language friction, airport-to-hotel simplicity. How quickly you can feel competent in a new place.
