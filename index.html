The Cougar Confidence Index

Our proprietary destination scoring system — built for grown women, by grown women.

Every destination earns a Cougar Confidence Index score from 1–100, based on nine factors that matter most to women who travel with self-knowledge and standards.

  • Respect for Women How women are treated in public spaces
  • Safety Reality Street safety, harassment patterns, scam risk
  • Social Ease How easy it is to meet people naturally
  • Cultural Depth Museums, neighborhoods, history, art
  • Wellness Access Spas, walkability, slow-life infrastructure
  • Solo-Friendliness Dining alone, tours, everyday friendliness
  • Age Inclusivity How 40/50/60+ women are perceived and welcomed
  • Healthcare Access Quality, accessibility, practical info
  • Ease Score Walkability, transit confidence, language friction
