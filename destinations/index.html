How We Score

Every destination on CougarTravel is evaluated using the Cougar Confidence Index (CCI) — a proprietary scoring system built specifically for women 40 and over. The CCI rates each city on a scale of 1 to 100 across nine factors that actually matter: Respect for Women, Safety Reality, Social Ease, Cultural Depth, Wellness Access, Solo-Friendliness, Age Inclusivity, Healthcare Access, and Ease Score.

These are not pulled from tourism board brochures. They are grounded in real conditions, real experiences, and an honest assessment of how a city treats a confident woman traveling on her own terms. No inflation, no sugarcoating, no participation trophies.
