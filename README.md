## 4) Signature IP: Cougar Confidence Index (Core Product)

Each destination receives a **Cougar Confidence Index (CCI)** score.

### CCI Score (1–100)
A composite “how good is this place for grown women?” score.

### Supporting Subscores (Optional but recommended)
- **Safety Score (1–10)**
- **Ease Score (1–10)** *(solo-friendliness, walkability, transit confidence)*

CCI is the moat. It must remain honest.

---

## 5) Cougar Confidence Index (Canonical Factors)

Every destination must be evaluated on:

- **Respect for Women** (how women are treated in public spaces)
- **Safety Reality** (street safety, harassment patterns, scam risk)
- **Social Ease** (how easy it is to meet people naturally)
- **Cultural Depth** (museums, neighborhoods, history, art)
- **Flirt Energy** *(opt-in; never central)*
- **Wellness Access** (spas, walkability, slow-life infrastructure)
- **Solo-Friendliness** (dining alone, tours, friendliness)
- **Age Inclusivity** (how 40/50/60+ women are perceived and included)
- **Healthcare Access** (quality + accessibility, practical info)

### Philosophy
- Safety and respect can override “fun.”
- Confidence is created by clarity.
- The best places feel **easy** and **welcoming** without demanding youth.

## 7) Canonical Destination Page Template (Exact Order)

Every destination page must follow this structure.

### Above the Fold (Required)
- Destination name + country
- Cougar Confidence Index (1–100)
- Safety Score (1–10)
- Ease Score (1–10)
- 3–6 “why it works” bullets
- One-line truth statement

Example:
> “Elegant, walkable, culturally rich — and grown women are treated with respect.”

---

### Section 1 — Who This Destination Is Perfect For
Specific archetypes:
- solo traveler
- friend group
- post-divorce reset
- slow traveler
- culture-first traveler
- queer traveler (when relevant, respectfully)

### Section 2 — Safety Reality (Adult, Calm)
Must include:
- walking-at-night reality by neighborhood
- common scams targeting women
- transit safety
- harassment patterns (if relevant)
- what locals do to stay safe

### Section 3 — Where to Stay (Neighborhoods That Work)
- best neighborhoods
- which areas to avoid (clear, factual)
- why each neighborhood fits the CougarTravel audience

### Section 4 — Hotels for Confident Women (Shortlist)
Hotels must be selected for:
- safe location
- calm comfort
- staff respect
- good sound/bed quality
- easy transit access
- not youth-hostel energy
- not family-chaos-heavy

### Section 5 — Cougar-Approved Experiences
Curate across:
- food & wine
- art & culture
- wellness & spa
- nature + soft adventure
- beach + swimming (when relevant)
- learning travel (language, cooking, history)
- dance (salsa/tango/etc.)

### Section 6 — Solo But Not Lonely (How to Socialize Naturally)
- where to sit
- how to meet people without “dating” energy
- tours/classes that attract interesting adults
- dining alone with confidence

### Section 7 — Best Times to Go (Comfort & Flow)
- weather comfort
- crowd seasons
- local calendars that matter

### Section 8 — Healthcare & Practical Reality
- access level and quality notes (high-level)
- pharmacies and emergency norms (practical, not medical advice)

### Section 9 — Cost & Ease Snapshot
- typical daily spend ranges (broad tiers)
- transport usability
- language friction

### Section 10 — Quick Summary Card (Bottom)
- CCI score
- Safety score
- Ease score
- best neighborhoods
- best trip length (3–5 days vs 7–10)
- “who should avoid” note (if applicable)

## 8) Hotels for Confident Women (Selection Rules)

Hotel content must be high-trust.

### Hotels must be:
- safe at night (location matters)
- comfortable and quiet

- “spring break” energy
